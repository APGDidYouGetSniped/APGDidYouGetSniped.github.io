APG RECRUITING PROCESS

The APG squad is not like other squads. Advanced Player Guild was created in October of 2021. Since then we have expanded. We are looking for players who are respectful and kind. If you get muted or banned while in the squad you will be cut.

There is a max of 32 players per squad so join while you still can. It is highly recommended that you play in either the USA, USA West, or Europe servers. If you don’t that's fine but it will be harder to join.
