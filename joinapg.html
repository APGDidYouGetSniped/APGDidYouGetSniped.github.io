[APG] Recruiting Process

The APG squad is not like other squads. Advanced Player Guild was created in October of 2021. Since then we have expanded. We are looking for players who are respectful and kind. If you get muted or banned while in the squad you will be cut.

There is a max of 32 players per squad so join while you still can. It is highly recommended that you play in either the USA, USA West, or Europe servers. If you don’t that's fine but it will be harder to join.

STEP 1a

Join the Discord

STEP 1b

Look over the following and see if you meet the requirements

Squad Name: Advanced Player Guild

Squad Tag: [APG]

Squad Leader: [APG] Did You Get Sniped?

Active Regions: USA, USA West, Europe

Requirements:

  1. Must be Level 50 or higher
  2. Must have a 2 k/d or higher
  3. Must have 10,000 kills or more
  4. Play Often
  5. English speaking squad
  6. (Note: You can do a playtest to join as well.)

If you meet them please go to step 2a.

If you don't meet them please go to step 2b.

STEP 2a

If you meet the requirements fill out this form

STEP 2b

Please request to have a playtest if you feel like you are better than what your stats represent.
